# 🎮 Game Taxonomy Knowledge Base (Expanded)

## 1. Primary Genres & Expectations
*   **Action**: Reflexes, Timing, Mastery.
    *   *Keywords*: Hitbox, I-Frames, DPS, Stun.
*   **Adventure**: Exploration, Puzzle, Narrative.
    *   *Keywords*: Backtracking, Key Items, Lore, Environmental Storytelling.
*   **RPG**: Progression, Customization, Numbers.
    *   *Keywords*: XP, Loot, Build, Turn-Economy, Min-Maxing.
*   **Strategy**: Planning, Resource Management, control.
    *   *Keywords*: Macro/Micro, Economy, Fog of War, Unit Composition.

## 2. The "Zelda-Like" Archetype (Action-Adventure)
*   **Core Loop**: Explore -> Solve Puzzle -> Combat -> Reward.
*   **Hook Variations**:
    *   *The Engineer*: Build gadgets to solve puzzles.
    *   *The Pacifist*: Deflect/Trap enemies instead of killing.
    *   *The Morph*: Transform into enemies to use their powers.
    *   *The Timelord*: Manipulate time to solve rot/decay puzzles.

## 3. Visual & Aesthetic Styles (The Vibe)
*   **GameBoy (Nostalgia)**: 4 Colors, 160x144, Chip-tune.
*   **Dark Fantasy (Souls)**: Decay, Ruins, High contrast, oppressive atmosphere.
*   **Cozy (Wholesome)**: Pastel colors, round shapes, no urgency, relaxing music.
*   **Cyberpunk (Neon)**: High tech, low life, neon lights, rain, synthwave.

## 4. Constraint Patterns (Scope Control)
*   **One-Room**: The entire game happens in one changing room.
*   **Boss Rush**: No exploration, just a series of complex fights.
*   **Time Loop**: The same 5 minutes play out differently (Outer Wilds).
*   **Single Mechanic**: Only ONE verb (e.g., "Jump"). Everything else is automatic.
